About U-RISE

We're changing the recruitment landscape in East Africa and beyond by putting job seekers first.

Our Mission

"Empowering jobseekers. Transforming Recruitment in East Africa and beyond."

U-RISE is Uganda's first reverse-recruiting platform. Unlike traditional agencies, we work directly for job seekers, acting as their personal career agents to manage the entire job search process.

We tackle the challenges of high youth unemployment, intense competition, and the hidden job market by providing comprehensive support from personal branding to expert coaching.

Our Services

We provide a comprehensive suite of services to empower your job search journey.

Personal Branding

We create compelling CVs, cover letters, and LinkedIn profiles that showcase your unique value proposition to potential employers.

Market Access

We provide access to both public and hidden job opportunities, giving you an edge over the competition.

Application Management

We apply to dozens of relevant jobs on your behalf, saving you time and increasing your chances of success.

Expert Coaching

We provide elite interview preparation and salary negotiation coaching to help you secure the best offers.

Skills Development

Training programs focused on digital literacy, communication, and professional readiness.

Regional Job Outreach Programs

We extend career and job placement opportunities to communities across Uganda through outreach initiatives.
