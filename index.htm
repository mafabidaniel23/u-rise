About U-RISE

We're changing the recruitment landscape in East Africa and beyond by putting job seekers first.

Our Mission

"Empowering jobseekers. Transforming Recruitment in East Africa and beyond."

U-RISE is Uganda's first reverse-recruiting platform. Unlike traditional agencies, we work directly for job seekers, acting as their personal career agents to manage the entire job search process.

We tackle the challenges of high youth unemployment, intense competition, and the hidden job market by providing comprehensive support from personal branding to expert coaching.

U-RISE CAREERS Ltd. - Client Terms and Conditions

×

These Terms and Conditions govern the provision of reverse-recruiting and career agency services by U-RISE CAREERS Ltd. to the client (the job seeker).

1. Scope of Service (Our Role)

1.1 Reverse-Recruiting Model: U-RISE CAREERS operates as a reverse-recruiting platform. We explicitly represent the job seeker as our client, functioning as their dedicated career agent.

1.2 Service Deliverables: Our services encompass managing the entire job search process, including:

  • Personal Brand Building: Creation or optimization of CVs, cover letters, and professional social media profiles (e.g., LinkedIn).
  • Job Search and Application: Actively searching and applying for jobs on the client's behalf, including access to hidden job market opportunities.
  • Coaching and Negotiation: Providing coaching for interviews and assisting with salary negotiation.

1.3 Target Audience: Services are designed for and targeted towards fresh graduates/entry-level professionals, mid-career individuals seeking better opportunities, and professionals re-entering the workforce.

2. Fees and Payment Model

2.1 Initial Service Fee: Clients shall pay a flat fee of UGX 50,000 for basic services to initiate the career agency relationship. This fee is non-refundable.

2.2 Success Fee: The primary fee structure is based on results. A success fee will become due and payable once the client is employed.

2.3 Success Fee Rate: The success fee rate is defined in the client's separate service agreement but will be structured as a percentage of the client's salary over an initial period, for example, 10% for the first 6 months of employment.

3. Client Expectations and Guarantees

3.1 No Guarantee of Placement: The client acknowledges that U-RISE CAREERS is responsible for maximizing opportunity and preparation, but job placement is not guaranteed. Services are rendered to maximize the client's chances of success in a competitive environment.

3.2 Client Cooperation: The client must fully cooperate with U-RISE CAREERS agents, providing timely information, attending coaching sessions, and acting professionally during all stages of the recruitment process.

4. Data Usage and Privacy

4.1 Data Monetization: The client acknowledges that U-RISE CAREERS utilizes a data monetization revenue stream.

4.2 Aggregated Insights: Client data (including career progression, salary levels, and job search behavior) may be collected, aggregated, and sold as market insights to employers and research firms. This data will be anonymized and presented in aggregate form, ensuring individual client identity is protected when sold commercially.

5. Termination and Governing Law

5.1 Termination: Either party may terminate the agreement according to terms outlined in the client's specific service contract.

5.2 Governing Law and Jurisdiction: These terms shall be governed by the laws of Uganda and shall be adjudicated in the relevant jurisdiction within East Africa.
